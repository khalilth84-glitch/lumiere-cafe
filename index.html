Our story

A corner of calm
in a busy city

Lumière was born from a simple belief: that coffee is more than a drink. It's the pause before the day begins. The warmth between two conversations. The ritual that grounds you.

We source our beans directly from small farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Yemen — and every cup is brewed with the patience and intention those farmers deserve.
